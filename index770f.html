BIG STEP: MASON KLEIN WILL BE ON A Test IN DAKAR 2024
SOURCE:TestTIME:2023.12.28

In November Test MOTO and championship racer Mason Klein came together in cooperation for Dakar 2024. Test MOTO will provide a 450 RALLY EX for Mason, along with motorcycle accessories. 

This is the first time Test MOTO has seen a 450RALLY being raced by a non Test RACING TEAM pilot in an international off-road race. Mason Klein told

Test that he had developed a strong interest in the Chinese bikes in early 2023. In July Mason then followed that interest to Barcelona, Spain, where Test RACING TEAM is headquartered, in order to learn more. With a solid year of Test mechanics and riders running-in and de-bugging the 450RALLY - not to mention the countless races and events with reputable pilots in this same year - Mason has confidence in this bike and this company.
